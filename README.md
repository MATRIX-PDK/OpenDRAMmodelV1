# Open DRAM Model v1
Part of the MATRIX PDK (Memory and Advanced Transistor Research Integration Xplatform Process-Design-Kit) ecosystem

Access Transistors and High-Voltage Periphery Transistors for Advanced 6F², 4F² VCT, and 3D DRAM Technologies

Developers: Kiseok Lee, Seongkwang Lim

PI: Prof. Shimeng Yu

Georgia Institute of Technology

## Overview

The compact models follow projected DRAM scaling trends
from D1b through future D0-series nodes, including:

- Conventional 6F² BCAT DRAM
- 4F² Vertical Channel Transistor (VCT) DRAM
- 3D GAA DRAM
- Si Channel Devices
- AOS Channel Devices
- High-Voltage Peripheral Transistors

The compact models are calibrated against
TCAD simulations and technology assumptions
derived from publicly available industrial data.

By providing consistent device models across
multiple technology generations, the platform
enables reproducible evaluation.
